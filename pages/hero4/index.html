Luffy Gear 4

Description: Luffy compresses his muscles and bones, giving him a more muscular and streamlined appearance.

Visual Effect: His body gains a hardened, armored look with steam emitting from his muscles.

Abilities: Enhances Luffy's strength, speed, and durability to an extreme level. It also grants him the ability to use new techniques like the "Boundman" form, where he combines his rubbery elasticity with Haki (a form of spiritual energy) to deliver powerful attacks.
