Luffy Gear 1

Description: Gear 1 represents Luffy's foundational use of stretching techniques and combat maneuvers enabled by the Gum-Gum Fruit. It emphasizes the basic application of his rubber-like powers in combat.

Visual Effect: There is no distinct visual transformation or aura associated with Gear 1. It showcases Luffy's elastic stretches and attacks, such as the "Gomu Gomu no Pistol" and "Gomu Gomu no Gatling," where his limbs extend and retract with rubbery elasticity.

Abilities: Gear 1 enhances Luffy's physical capabilities by allowing him to stretch and manipulate his body like rubber. This grants him extended reach, flexibility, and the ability to deliver powerful punches and kicks. Despite being his initial Gear form, Gear First establishes the core of Luffy's combat style, emphasizing agility, resilience, and strategic use of his rubber abilities in battle.
